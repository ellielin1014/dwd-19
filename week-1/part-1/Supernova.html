Supernova

Supernova is an event that occurs upon the death of certain types of stars.

In Latin, nova means "new", referring astronomically to what appears to be a temporary new bright star.

Classification

  • Type I < No Hydrogen >
  • Type II < Shows Hydrogen >
  • Type III, IV and V
